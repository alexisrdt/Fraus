Fraus

What is Fraus?

Fraus is a rendering engine based on Vulkan. It is written in C(99) to maximize portability and match Vulkan's requirements.

Capabilities

Fraus is still in development. It is currently able to render a textured 3D model.

Plans

I am working on adding first person camera controls.
I will then work on making it possible to render multiple objects at once.
After that, I may try to add a GUI or explore ray tracing / compute shaders.
